# Mizu Care

## Product Vision

**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** Draft
**Product Owner:** Carol
**Primary Users:** Bryan, Carol, Nely, Liby, Ash, Nomi, Ovi

---

# 1. Vision Statement

Mizu Care is a mobile-first Progressive Web App (PWA) that helps our family consistently care for our dog, Mizu, by providing a shared schedule, accountability, reminders, and historical tracking.

The application should reduce uncertainty around who is responsible for each task, encourage participation through light gamification, and create a permanent record of Mizu's growth and milestones.

The app should feel simple enough for children to use independently while remaining powerful enough for adults to manage schedules, history, analytics, and long-term care.

---

# 2. Product Goals

The application should:

* Ensure every scheduled responsibility is visible and easy to complete.
* Clearly indicate who is responsible for each task.
* Record who completed every task and when.
* Provide fair participation tracking through a point system.
* Encourage consistency without creating unnecessary complexity.
* Help identify patterns in Mizu's routine through analytics.
* Preserve memories of Mizu's growth through a timeline and journal.

---

# 3. Guiding Principles

### Family First

The application should reduce conversations such as "Who was supposed to do that?" by making responsibilities visible and transparent.

---

### Mobile First

Every workflow should be completable comfortably using one hand on a phone.

No interaction should require precision clicking or excessive typing.

---

### Fast

Completing a scheduled task should take less than five seconds.

Logging a potty accident should take less than five seconds.

Viewing today's schedule should require no more than one tap.

---

### Beautiful

The interface should feel calm, modern, and welcoming.

Design language:

* Soft rounded corners
* Minimal visual clutter
* Friendly typography
* Subtle paw-print accents
* Smooth animations
* Gentle colors

---

### Reliable

The schedule should never lose completed tasks.

History should always be recoverable.

Offline operation should continue whenever possible.

---

### Extensible

The architecture should support future additions such as:

* Multiple dogs
* Medication tracking
* Vet records
* Calendar synchronization
* Home screen widgets
* Apple Watch support

without requiring major redesign.

---

# 4. Success Metrics

The product is considered successful if it achieves the following:

* Family members consistently complete scheduled responsibilities.
* Daily completion rate exceeds 90%.
* Sign-offs accurately identify who completed each task.
* The application becomes the family's primary source of truth for Mizu's care.
* Growth journal entries continue over multiple years.
* The application remains intuitive for both adults and children.

# 7. Product Personality

The application should feel like:

"A calm, organized family assistant."

It should never feel like:

* A corporate productivity tool
* A complicated veterinary system
* A game that overshadows Mizu's care

Gamification exists only to encourage participation.

---

# 8. Core Experience

Every day should follow a simple rhythm:

1. Open the app.
2. See the current task.
3. Complete the responsibility.
4. Sign off.
5. Hear a satisfying completion sound.
6. Watch progress increase.
7. Celebrate a fully completed day.

The application should become a trusted daily companion rather than another chore.

---

# 9. Long-Term Vision

Over time, Mizu Care should become more than a task manager.

It should become a digital family memory book containing:

* Growth photos
* Weight history
* Milestones
* Grooming history
* Potty training progress
* Family participation
* Special memories

Years from now, the application should allow the family to look back and relive Mizu's journey from puppyhood through adulthood.

---

# 10. Definition of Success

A successful version of Mizu Care is one that:

* Is trusted by every family member.
* Is opened multiple times every day.
* Makes responsibilities clear.
* Eliminates uncertainty about completed tasks.
* Encourages teamwork.
* Documents Mizu's life in a meaningful and lasting way.
